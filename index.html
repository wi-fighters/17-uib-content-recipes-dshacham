Vegan Chocolate Balls

Ingredients:

  • 400g Lotus Biscoff cookies (or other cookies)
  • 100g baking chocolate
  • 8 tablespoons sugar
  • 10 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup soy milk
  • ground coconut

Preparation:

  1. crumble the cookies into tiny pieces
  2. melt the chocolate, add the sugar and let it melt
  3. pour the chocolate-sugar mix on the cookie crumbles
  4. add the cocoa powder and soy milk
  5. mix until you get a dense dough
  6. with wet hands make balls (if they're not wet it sticks to the hands)
  7. put the ground cocounut on a plate and coat the balls by rolling them over it
  8. put in freezer for 30-60 minutes
